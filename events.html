Easter Sunday Celebration

📅 Sunday, 20th April 2025
🕗 8:00 AM & 10:30 AM
📍 Main Sanctuary, TFBCI
👥 All are welcome

Join us as we celebrate the resurrection of our Lord Jesus Christ in a special Easter Sunday service. Two services will hold — come and bring a friend, a neighbour, someone who needs to hear the Good News. Our choir and music team will lead us in worship fit for this glorious day.
