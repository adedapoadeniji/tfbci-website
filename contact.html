First Time?

Plan Your Visit

We want your first visit to be a wonderful experience. Here's what to expect when you come to The First Baptist Church Ijaye.

👋

You Are Welcome

Come as you are. Our welcome team will be at the entrance to greet you, answer questions, and help you find your seat. No dress code — just come.

🕗

Arrive a Little Early

We recommend arriving 10–15 minutes before service. Sunday School begins before the main service — you're welcome to join that too.

🧒

Bringing Children?

We have a dedicated Children's Ministry running during the Sunday service with trained teachers. Your children are safe and will enjoy it.

🅿️

Parking

Parking is available on the church premises and along the street. Ushers are on hand to assist with parking on busy Sundays.

🙏

After the Service

First-time visitors are invited to meet the Pastor and team after service. Refreshments are occasionally served — stay and fellowship with us.

❓

Have Questions?

Use the contact form above or speak to any of our ushers. No question is too small — we're here to serve you.
