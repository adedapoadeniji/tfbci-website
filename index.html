Nestled in the heart of Abeokuta, The First Baptist Church Ijaye has been a beacon of hope, love, and service to our community. We are a family of believers committed to the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Whether you are searching for faith, returning to church, or looking for a spiritual home — you will find open arms and an open Word here. Come as you are.
