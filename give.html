What Your Gift Supports

Types of Giving

At TFBCI, we receive various forms of giving. Use the offering envelope or indicate when making a transfer which fund your gift is directed to.

🙏

Tithes

A tenth of your income returned to God as an act of obedience and worship (Malachi 3:10).

💝

Freewill Offering

A voluntary gift given cheerfully from your heart to support the general work of the church.

🏗️

Building Fund

Dedicated giving towards the construction, renovation, and maintenance of church facilities.

📢

Missions & Evangelism

Supporting the church's evangelism outreaches and missionary efforts in the community.

❤️

Welfare Fund

Providing support and benevolence to members and community members in genuine need.

🎓

Scholarship Fund

Supporting the education of children and young people from the church and wider community.

🎵

Music Ministry

Funding instruments, sound equipment, and choir resources for Spirit-filled worship.

🌟

Special Projects

One-off designated giving for specific church projects as announced by the leadership.
