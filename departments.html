Music & Choir

The Music and Choir department leads TFBCI in heartfelt, Spirit-filled worship that ushers the congregation into the presence of God. Our choir and instrumentalists serve faithfully every Sunday and at special events.

  • Sunday worship leading at both services
  • Special seasonal cantatas and concerts
  • Weekly choir rehearsals on Saturdays
  • Instrumental ensemble and praise team ministry
  • Participation in Ogun Baptist Conference events

Children's Ministry

We believe that children are not just the church of tomorrow — they are the church of today. Our Children's Ministry provides a safe, joyful, and Bible-centred environment where children discover Jesus and grow in faith.

  • Age-appropriate Bible teaching every Sunday
  • Children's choir and drama ministry
  • Vacation Bible School during holidays
  • Children's Day celebrations and events
  • Dedicated, trained and vetted teachers

Young & On Fire

Youth Department

The Youth Department of TFBCI is a vibrant, Spirit-led community of young people committed to pursuing God, building character, and making an impact. We meet every Saturday and run empowerment programmes throughout the year.

  • Weekly Saturday fellowship at 4:00 PM
  • Annual Youth Empowerment Summit
  • Youth Week of Excellence (June)
  • Career, academic and spiritual mentoring
  • Community service and evangelism outreaches
  • Representation at NBC Youth conventions

Men's Fellowship

The Men's Fellowship — also known as the Men's Missionary Union (MMU) — brings together the men of TFBCI to grow in godliness, build meaningful brotherhood, and serve God's kingdom with strength and integrity.

  • Monthly fellowship and prayer meetings
  • Men's retreat and discipleship programmes
  • Church maintenance and building projects
  • Mentoring of younger men and youth
  • Family and marriage enrichment initiatives
  • Representation at NBC MMU programmes

Strength & Grace

Women's Missionary Society

The Women's Missionary Union (WMU) of TFBCI is a community of women walking together in faith, prayer, and service. We celebrate the strength, wisdom and grace of women in the body of Christ and in our community.

  • Monthly fellowship, prayer and Bible study
  • Women's retreat and spiritual formation programmes
  • Support for widows, single mothers and the vulnerable
  • Mentoring and discipleship for younger women
  • Welfare and benevolence ministry
  • Representation at NBC WMU conferences

Regular Outreaches

E — Evangelism is Our Main Focus

Evangelism Department

Evangelism is not just a department — it is at the heart of who we are as a church. Rooted in the IJAYE core value that "Evangelism is our main focus," this team takes the Good News of Jesus Christ into the streets, markets, homes, and institutions of Abeokuta.

  • Regular market and street evangelism outreaches
  • Hospital and prison visitation ministry
  • Follow-up and discipleship of new converts
  • Door-to-door outreach in Ijaye and environs
  • Collaboration with NBC evangelism programmes
  • Training of members in personal evangelism

Rooted in the Word

Sunday School

Sunday School at TFBCI is a rich, systematic Bible study programme for all ages, conducted before the main service each Sunday. It forms the educational backbone of our congregation — grounding members in the Scriptures and equipping them to live out their faith.

  • Weekly Sunday School before the morning service
  • Separate classes for adults, youth, and children
  • NBC-approved Sunday School curriculum
  • Annual Sunday School Day celebration
  • Teacher training and development programmes
  • Scripture memorisation and Bible quiz competitions

Compassion in Action

Welfare & Care

The Welfare and Care department embodies the church's mission to share Christ's love by meeting the physical, emotional and practical needs of our members and the wider community. No one should walk through hardship alone.

  • Visiting the sick and hospitalised members
  • Bereavement support and funeral coordination
  • Support for elderly and shut-in members
  • Emergency benevolence fund for members in need
  • Coordination of community relief outreaches
  • Liaison with church leadership on pastoral care
